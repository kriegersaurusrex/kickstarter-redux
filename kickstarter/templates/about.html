ABOUT KICKSTARTER- SUCESS

Over time, we have found that certain characteristics make one

kick starter, more likely to succeed than the other.

In this project, we have decided to utilize Machine Learning to

take a deep dive into the Kick starter world and see if we can predict

to a decent degree of accuracy, the chances of a kikstarter succeeding

or failing
